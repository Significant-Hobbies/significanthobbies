# Hobby blog keyword research & content plan

> Durable reference for blog content planning. Feeds `src/lib/blog-posts.ts`
> and the `/blog` routes.

**Date:** 2026-03-15
**Purpose:** Identify high-volume, searchable blog topics related to hobbies for organic traffic acquisition.

---

## Search Volume Reference Key

- **Very High**: 100K+ monthly searches (estimated)
- **High**: 30K-100K monthly searches
- **Medium**: 10K-30K monthly searches
- **Low-Medium**: 5K-10K monthly searches
- **Low**: Under 5K monthly searches

## Competition Reference Key

- **High**: Dominated by major publications (Indeed, Healthline, WebMD, major media)
- **Medium**: Mix of authority sites and niche blogs ranking
- **Low**: Niche blogs and smaller sites can rank with quality content

## Content Strategy Recommendations

### Publishing Priority Order

**Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Foundational Pillar Posts**
Publish the Tier 1 posts first. These are the highest-volume keywords and will serve as pillar content that all other posts link back to:
- "List of Hobbies" (the mega-list)
- "How to Find a Hobby" (direct app funnel)
- "Hobbies That Make Money"
- "Things to Do When Bored"

**Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): Demographic Targeting**
- "Hobbies for Men"
- "Hobbies for Women"
- "Hobbies for Couples"
- "Hobbies for Teens"
- "Hobbies for Introverts"

**Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12): Mental Health and Lifestyle**
- "Hobbies for Anxiety"
- "Hobbies for ADHD"
- "How to Be More Interesting"
- "Hobbies for Lazy People"
- "Best Hobbies for Mental Health"

**Phase 4 (Ongoing): Long-Tail and Seasonal**
- Publish seasonal posts 6-8 weeks before the season begins
- Publish "how to start X" posts as a series
- Publish comparison posts (yoga vs pilates, etc.) as needed

### Internal Linking Strategy

Every blog post should:
1. Link to the "How to Find a Hobby" post (which links to the app quiz)
2. Link to 3-5 related blog posts (e.g., "Hobbies for Introverts" links to "Hobbies for Anxiety" and "Indoor Hobbies")
3. Include a CTA to the Significant Hobbies app for hobby discovery and profile building
4. Cross-link to relevant hobby profile pages on the platform

### Content Format Best Practices

Based on what currently ranks for hobby keywords:
- **Numbered lists** dominate SERPs (e.g., "101 Hobbies for Men")
- **Use odd or specific numbers** in titles (127 beats 100, 43 beats 40)
- **Include the year** in titles for freshness signals
- **Add comparison tables** where relevant
- **Include FAQ sections** targeting People Also Ask queries
- **Feature images** showing people doing the hobbies (not just text)
- **Add "how to start" mini-sections** within list posts for key hobbies

### Conversion Funnel Integration

Each blog post should funnel readers toward:
1. Taking the hobby personality quiz on the app
2. Creating a Significant Hobbies profile
3. Exploring hobby pages on the platform
4. Sharing their hobby profile or quiz results

### Quick-Win Keywords (Low Competition, Direct App Relevance)

These are the most immediately rankable keywords that directly serve the app's value proposition:
- "How to find a hobby" (direct funnel)
- "Hobby quiz" / "What hobby should I try" (quiz funnel)
- "Midlife crisis hobbies" (low competition, high emotional intent)
- "Hobbies for lonely people" (low competition, community angle)
- "Quarter life crisis hobbies" (low competition, Gen Z target)
- "Hobbies for empty nesters" (low competition, underserved)
- "Hobbies that make you smarter" (low competition, shareable)
